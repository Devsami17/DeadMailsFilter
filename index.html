About Our Founders

Our founders have a rich background in digital marketing and a first hand understanding of the challenges faced by businesses with extensive email lists. After managing a successful web business with over a million subscribers, they were taken aback to discover that cleaning their email list could cost more than $20,000 a year. Determined to find a better solution, they decided to invest that money into developing a platform that offers high-quality email list cleaning services at a fraction of what the industry charges.

With a mission to help others like themselves, they are committed to providing the lowest prices on the market-at least 10% lower than competitors-while ensuring accuracy and reliability. Additionally, we prioritize customer privacy and security, guaranteeing that we do not save or sell any of your data. Our goal is to empower businesses to maintain clean email lists without overspending or compromising their data integrity.
